Topics:

  1. Geometric optics may include the following topics:
    1. a) Wave fronts and rays (Huygens’s Principle)
    2. b) Reflection (Law of reflection, specular reflection, diffuse reflection)
    3. c) Refraction (measurement of index of refraction, Snell’s Law)
    4. d) Critical Angle (measurement of critical angle, fiber optics)
    5. e) Dispersion Due to a Prism
    6. f) Simple Lens and mirrors (ray tracing, measurement of focal length,  mirror equation, thin lens equation, magnification, lens maker’s equation)
  2. Physical Optics may include the following topics:
    1. a) Interference and superposition of waves
    2. b) Double Slit Interference (Young’s experiment--location of peaks only, not intensity)
    3. c) Lasers (theory of operation, difference between coherent and non-coherent light)
    4. d) Electromagnetic Spectrum
      1. 1) wavelength, frequency, velocity, nomenclature of EM spectrum,
      2. 2) inverse square law applied to the intensity of light
      3. 3) Doppler Shift,
      4. 4) Energy and momentum of photons,
      5. 5) Bright Line Spectra,
      6. 6) Absorption Spectra
